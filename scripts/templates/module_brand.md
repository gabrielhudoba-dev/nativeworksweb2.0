B1. SCOPE

This Schedule covers the creation of a brand identity system as described in the Proposal, including logo design, visual identity system, and brand guidelines.

B2. DELIVERABLES

     (a) Logo design: primary logo, secondary/icon variant, monochrome variant
     (b) Colour palette (primary, secondary, neutral scales) with HEX, RGB, CMYK, and Pantone references
     (c) Typography selection and hierarchy specification
     (d) Brand guidelines document (PDF and/or Figma source)
     (e) Final file delivery in formats: SVG, PDF, PNG (transparent), WEBP, EPS
     (f) Figma design file (editable source) transferred to Client's Figma account

B3. NOT INCLUDED (REQUIRES CHANGE ORDER)

     — Trademark search, registration, or legal clearance (Client assumes all risk for trademark conflicts)
     — Brand naming or tagline development
     — Print production, file preparation for specific printers
     — Motion / animated logo variants
     — Social media template design
     — Brand implementation across existing collateral
     — Photography or custom illustration

B5. TRADEMARK DISCLAIMER

Native Works does not conduct trademark clearance searches. Client is solely responsible for ensuring that the brand identity created under this Schedule does not infringe existing trademarks. Native Works accepts no liability for any trademark infringement claims arising from Client's use of the delivered identity.

B6. REVISION ROUNDS

     Concept stage:     Three (3) initial concepts presented; Client selects one direction
     Development:       Two (2) rounds of revisions to selected concept
     Refinement:        One (1) round of revisions to final direction
     File delivery:     Not subject to revision (technical format changes excepted)

B7. APPROVAL AND LOCK

Once Client approves the final identity direction, subsequent changes to the approved direction constitute a new scope and require a Change Order. Minor refinements (colour brightness, spacing adjustments within the approved aesthetic) are included in the refinement round.
