SERVICE SCHEDULE — UX / PRODUCT DESIGN

Annexed to SOW Ref: {{sow_ref}}

─────────────────────────────────────────────────

U1. SCOPE

This Schedule covers user experience research, interaction design, and product design as described in the Proposal, which may include discovery research, user journey mapping, wireframing, prototyping, and high-fidelity interface design.

U2. DELIVERABLES

     (a) Discovery: user research synthesis, competitive analysis, and/or design audit (as specified in Proposal)
     (b) Information architecture and user journey maps
     (c) Wireframes (low-fidelity) for agreed flows and screens
     (d) Interactive prototype (Figma or equivalent) for user testing or stakeholder review
     (e) High-fidelity UI designs for agreed screens (responsive: desktop and mobile unless otherwise specified)
     (f) Design system or component library (if specified in Proposal)
     (g) Design handoff file with annotated specifications for development

U3. NOT INCLUDED (REQUIRES CHANGE ORDER)

     — User research involving recruitment and moderated testing sessions (unless specified in Proposal)
     — Accessibility audit to WCAG 2.1 AA level (unless specified)
     — Copy/content writing
     — Development implementation
     — Designs for platforms not specified in the Proposal
     — Motion design or animated transitions
     — Print or marketing collateral design

U4. RESEARCH AND INSIGHTS

Where discovery research is included, Native Works shall conduct the research using methods agreed in advance. Research findings and recommendations are advisory. Client retains full discretion over which recommendations to act upon.

U5. REVISION ROUNDS

     Discovery / research deliverables:  One (1) round of clarification questions
     Wireframes:                          Two (2) rounds of revisions
     High-fidelity designs:              Two (2) rounds of revisions per screen set
     Prototype:                           Revised after each revision round; no separate revision count

U6. DESIGN TOOL AND HANDOFF

     Primary design tool: Figma
     Handoff: Design file transferred to Client's Figma organisation upon final payment
     Specifications: All designs shall include spacing, typography, colour tokens, and interaction notes sufficient for a competent development team to implement

U7. OWNERSHIP OF RESEARCH ARTEFACTS

All research data, interview recordings (if any), and synthesis artefacts collected during this engagement are owned by Client. Native Works retains no right to use raw research data after project completion, other than for anonymised learning and methodology improvement.
