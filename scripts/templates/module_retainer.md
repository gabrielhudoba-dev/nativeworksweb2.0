SERVICE SCHEDULE — RETAINER / MAINTENANCE

─────────────────────────────────────────────────

R1. SCOPE

This Schedule governs an ongoing retainer engagement under which Native Works provides a pre-agreed number of hours per month of design, development, or strategic services to Client.

R2. RETAINER TERMS

     Monthly hours:        {{retainer_hours}} hours/month
     Monthly retainer fee: {{retainer_fee}} EUR (excl. VAT)
     Billing date:         1st of each calendar month, invoiced in advance
     Minimum term:         Three (3) months from commencement
     Notice period:        Sixty (60) days written notice to terminate after minimum term

R3. HOUR ALLOCATION

3.1  Unused hours expire at the end of each calendar month and do not roll over, unless otherwise agreed in writing.

3.2  Hours may be used for any service within Native Works' capabilities, subject to advance request via agreed project management channel.

3.3  Work requests must be submitted with reasonable advance notice: at least five (5) business days for tasks requiring more than four (4) hours; at least one (1) business day for tasks under four (4) hours.

3.4  Hours used beyond the monthly allocation are subject to a Change Order or invoiced at Native Works' current hourly rate of €{{hourly_rate}}, quoted and approved in advance.

R4. RESPONSE TIMES

     Standard tasks:      Commenced within two (2) business days of request
     Urgent tasks:        Commenced within one (1) business day (subject to availability)
     Emergency (critical production issues): Best effort within four (4) business hours during working hours (09:00–18:00 CET, Monday–Friday)

R5. SCOPE OF RETAINER

Services available under this retainer: design updates, feature additions, content management, bug fixes, third-party integrations, technical consultation, and such other services as Native Works can reasonably provide within its competencies.

Services outside scope (requiring a separate SOW): complete redesigns, new product launches, or projects requiring more than forty (40) hours of concentrated effort within a single month.

R6. MONTHLY REPORTING

Native Works shall provide a monthly summary of hours used, tasks completed, and remaining hours, delivered by the 5th of the following calendar month.

R7. TERMINATION

7.1  Either Party may terminate this retainer by providing sixty (60) days' written notice after the minimum term has elapsed.

7.2  Client shall pay all outstanding invoices on termination. Hours paid but unused as of the termination date shall be refunded on a pro-rated basis, calculated against the monthly fee, within fourteen (14) days.

7.3  In the event of Client's material breach (including non-payment), Native Works may terminate this retainer immediately upon written notice without refund obligation.
